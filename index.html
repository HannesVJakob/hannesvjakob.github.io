I am a postdoctoral researcher in set theory. Inside set theory, I am most interested in large cardinal axioms and consistency results obtained by forcing from large cardinal assumptions. After receiving my PhD from the University of Freiburg (Advisor: Heike Mildenberger), I am now a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of North Texas (Mentor: John Krueger). For more information, you can look at my CV or my PhD Thesis.
